The 8th ANNUAL HAWAII STATE OVERALL FLYING DISC CHAMPIONSHIPS
Sponsored by Hawaii Ultimate League Association and The Wright Life

    When:
  • Saturday, August 4th 9:00 AM
  • Sunday, August 5th 9:00 AM
    Where:
  • Saturday - Ho'omaluhia Botanical Gardens (Disc Golf, Discathon)
  • Sunday - Kapiolani Park Rugby Field (Double Disc Court, Accuracy)
    Costs:
  • None to current, dues-paid HULA members
  • None to neighbor island competitors
  • $10 for all others
    Events:
  • Disc Golf (2 rounds)
  • Discathon (1 race)
  • Accuracy (1 round/sudden-death tie-breaker, if necessary)
  • Double Disc Court(round-robin, semi's, finals)
Divisions: Open, Womenz, and Juniors (18 y.o. and younger)

Miscellaneous: juice, water, sodas will be provided; competitors are responsible for own lunch; bring your own water-bottles and chairs. Accuracy and DDC discs are supplied; bring your own golf discs (for disc golf) and 3 ultimate discs (for discathon).

Prizes: Gift certificates from the Wright Life Alternative Sports Store, Ft. Collins, CO. Wright Life catalogs will be provided to each winner. web-site: www.wrightlife.com

Prizes will be given for the following winners:
1st Place Open Overall ($40 certificate), 2nd Place Open Overall ($25 certificate), 3rd Place Open Overall ($15 certificate)
1st Place Womenz Overall ($40 certificate), 2nd Place Womenz Overall ($25 certificate), 3rd Place Womenz Overall ($15 certicate)
1st Place Open & Womenz Golf ($15 certificates), 1st Place Open & Womenz Accuracy ($15 certificates), 1st Place Open and Womenz DDC (partners) ($15 certificates); 1st Place Open & Womenz Discathon ($15 certificates)
Prizes will also be awarded for the Junior division

Overall Points will be awarded for participating in each event. Rules for these events may be found at www.wfdf.org
