To Poli Poli
From Kahului take Hana hwy 36 to Haleakala Hwy 37 right going up hill. continue on 37 straight through both lights. After Rice park take a left on hwy 377 and a quick right on Waipoli Rd. go up switchbacks until you come to a big grass area with firepit and trees.

Cafe 808
going towards course on 37 take left at community center sign on lower Kula rd. From course go back to 37 and a right on Copp Rd. take left at stop sign on lower kula rd.
