5th ANNUAL
FALL CO-ED HAT DRAW
AND CAMP OUT

Saturday, November 11, 2000
Waimanalo Polo Fields, Oahu, Hawaii


Format: We are going with an new Hat Draw format this year.
After each game some players will be traded to the other team by another hat draw.
The number of traded players will depend on attendance.
At the end of the day players with the highest number of wins will be chosen as the winners.

Fee: In the Spirit of Thanksgiving, please bring one or more non-perishable food items
for donation to Hawaii Foodbank.

# of Teams: We are planning on 6 teams

Games: to 10 or one hour hard cap
HULA will provide some juice, sodas, water and fruit for the day.


Schedule:
Players from the signup sheet will be chosen by hat draw at 10:00 AM
10:30 Game 1
11:45 Game 2
1:00 Lunch or Swim Break
1:30 Game 3
2:45 Game 4
4:00 Game 5

Campout:
We are going to try to secure the farthest 4 campsites at Bellows Beach Park
These will stretch from the road to the ocean.
At 6:00 HULA will provide food and drinks.
Potluck items are always appreciated.
