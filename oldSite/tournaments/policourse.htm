IMPORTANT RULES
Holes 4, 11, and 21 have relief drop areas which may be used anytime during play on
that hole, the designated Penalty stroke(s) shall be applied accordingly.

Any other lost disc must be played from the last playable lie within reason as
determined by the majority of the group plus one stroke penalty.

A player may at any time take relief from a woodpile when a shot and/or stance is
obstructed. Relief may not be any closer to the target and must be as close to origional
lie as possible. When on the fringe of a one meter relief is allowed.

At any time a player may choose relief w/ a one stroke penalty. The relief is 5 meters
(16 ft.) and no closer to the pin.

At any time a player may take relief back to the fairway with a two stroke penalty.
The spot shall be marked at the last reasonably playable spot the disc passed over as
determined by the group. Exept on holes 4, 11, and 21 which have designated drop zones
and penalties.

On hole #21 any player may choose to take a one stroke penalty and throw from
the designated relief area.
