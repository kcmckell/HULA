"Seven-in-the-Sun" Tournament

Sunday:June 23, 2002

Central Oahu Regional Park
.....for all of you who have ever lamented insufficient playing time.......this is the tournament for you.  A day in the BLAZING SUN and SOFT GRASS of Central Oahu Regional Park.
 
 
This savage seven's tournament will bring you all the disc you can take in a day.  Seven players per team, seven players on the line..... or as many can still stand.... and at least seven games to play.
