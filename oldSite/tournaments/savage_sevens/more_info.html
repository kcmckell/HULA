MORE INFORMATION
 

 

TIME and PLACE and SCHEDULE

Central Oahu Regional Park-Fields 4 and 5 (the usual league fields)

Rosters drawn at 8:30 am; FIRST PULL at 9 am Sunday June 23, 2002

At least 4 morning games followed by 1 hour lunch and 3 afternoon games

 

FORMAT

Hat draw tournament. Five men/two women on the field.

Round robin play, with at least seven games per team.

50 minute games with 10 minutes between games

1 hour lunch break (snacks and drinks provided)

New players welcome

 

REGISTRATION

Five dollars per person (for the cost of snacks and prizes)

Registration ends at 8:45 am on the day of tournament

Pay Mike Leech early to get a chance to win a prize at the League Finals Party
