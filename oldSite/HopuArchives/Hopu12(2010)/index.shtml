Who: You and up to 16 spirited co-ed teams

Why: Find the Booty of Waimanalo!


Bids & Registration Details

Team Bid Deadline: Team bids will be due on September 1st, 2010.
Team Bid Deposit: $309, which is credited towards three (3) individual registration fees.
Individual Registration Fees:
Early Bird Fee, Postmarked On or Before October 1st, 2010: $103
Fees will be raised after Oct. 1st, and again for Day-of registration (required) after Nov. 3rd
Guest Registration Fees: $20/day (no player's pack) or $60/weekend (includes player's pack) per guest. Guest fee includes breakfasts & dinners, and entertainment.
Refunds:
Full refund of team deposit requires (minus $5 for processing) 6 weeks notice (by October 1st).
Refund requests up to 2 weeks prior result in a penalty of one player's fee and the $5 processing fee (between October 1st and 30th).
Individual refunds can be made up to 2 weeks prior (before October 30th) minus a $5 processing fee.
