Pre Hopu Hat Draw

Where:
Hawi, Hawaii at the Kamehameha Park Complex.

When:
November 3 & 4, 2007 Sat and Sun

Cost:
$25/person

What:
Hat Draw! Mix and mingle with the folks you'll be playing against next weekend! You get 1.5 days of play, dinner, and a party in the barn. You get some extra time on Sunday to explore the awesome town of Hawi and the nearby sites and beaches.

No need to be playing Hopu, you can just play in the Hat if you like. Great for anyone over 16 (we have insurance issues) and all skill levels.

Housing:
After lots of searching, PJ has found us some killer cheap housing in Hawi town. The Kohala Village Inn should hook you up with some rooms. When registering, make reference to the "Ultimate Frisbee Group".
