All you need to know so far...

What: Hopu Ka Lewa 9
When: November 10- 12, 2007
Where: Waimanalo Polo Fields, Oahu, Hawai'i
Who: Up to 16 fun, spirited, co-ed teams
Why: Because we throw a hell of a party! Your registration fee covers nightly entertainment, camping accommodations, a Hopu disc, and the majority of the weekend meals.
Team Bid Deadline: Team bids will be due on September 15th, 2007.
Team Bid Deposit: $315, which is credited towards individual registration fees.
Individual Registration Fees:
Before October 15th, 2007: $105
After October 15th, 2007: $130
Guest Registration Fees: $60 per guest. Guest fee includes breakfasts & dinners, entertainment and player pack minus disc. Disc can be purchased at tournament if desired.
Refunds: Team and Individual Refunds will only be given if applied for before October 30th, 2007.
