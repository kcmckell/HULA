Bid Process and Fees

Rally your coed teams and come and play in beautiful Hawaii. We want teams with great spirit and willingness to have fun!

Team bids

*Team bids for Hopu 9 are due on September 15th, 2007*
Please contact TD for questions about team bids, as described below. Bids require:

  • A letter of introduction. Explain how your team can contribute to competitive play yet still maintain outstanding spirit and fun. We accept bribes of local beers or your local specialties. The spirit of HOPU has been built on Hawaiian Aloha, ultimate spirit, competitive play combine with high quality cheers and gifts, and last but not least the inter-tournament BOAT RACE tournament. We encourage team cheers and toasts at any time during the tournament, not just after you play. Bring on the Aloha! Additionally, at opening ceremonies each team is requested to give a cheer introducing their team and players. Start thinking.
  • A team roster. This is a coed tourney so please have enough men and women. We try to always play 4/3, please be prepared. (Disguises including boobs and skirts will be acceptable on a limited basis.)
  • Deposit of $315 per team. We only accept U.S. checks or money orders payable to "Hawaii Coed Ultimate". The bid deposit is credited towards individual registration fees (ie. your team bid deposit pays for three individuals).
