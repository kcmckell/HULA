Tourney Details

Logistical details from 2006 (to be updated April 2007)

Kickoff: Registration will occur at Waimanalo Polo Fields Friday evening between 6 pm and 9 pm. Field is on the mountain side of Kalanianaole Highway across from Bellows Beach.

Games: Fields are at the Waimanalo Polo Fields.

  • Opening Ceremony will be at 8:30am Saturday, November 10.
  • Saturday games 9am - roughly 5pm.
  • Sunday games 9am - roughly 3:30pm.
  • Monday will be semis at 9:30am and finals at 12:00 noon (approx).

Food: Breakfast and dinner will be provided for you at the fields.

  • Breakfast will be served 7-8am , pretty much continental fare.
  • Lunch is on your own; however, there will be a couple of local vendors at the fields for convenience. Bring money. Other options are in Waimanalo, a five minute drive.
  • Dinner will be served at 6:30 pm, and second servings will begin at 7:15 pm to allow for slow shower-ers to ensure they are fed.
  • Entertainment will follow both evenings.

Camping: Camping is provided from Friday through Monday nights at Waimanalo Bay Recreation Area, directly across the street from the fields (a 2 minute walk). You may shower at the campgrounds.



Travel details

Rental cars are available at the airport.

If you prefer not to camp, rental options are available, but you are on your own to secure them. Search near/under 'Waimanalo'. Honolulu is a 45 minute drive.

If you need assistance with housing before or after the tournament, please contact us and we will try to find floor space, yard space, or direct you to great camping.
