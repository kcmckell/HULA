Spring Tournaments and Honolulu Pickup
Kaimana 22 and the following Maui Hat Draw were both huge successes, destroying our bodies, minds and livers all for the sake of chasing plastic. Next up on O`ahu is yearly Spring League, starting in mid-March this year with a hat draw structure and fun games promised by the new commissioners Bill Voss and Jennifer Milholen. Many thanks to the two of them for organizing.

Tuesday pickup at Ala Moana Beach park has been fully revived with bribes of free beverages sponsored by HULA and the community. Thursday pickup will be starting again at a NEW LOCATION AND TIME at Kapaolono park under the lights after the first new rec night as well on March 12, 2009. Come stretch your legs and enjoy some lighthearted ultimate under the lights. The HULA board was hoping that by offering two timeslots (dusk on Tuesday and evening on Thursday) we'd be able to accommodate more players who can't make one of them. But I'm sure we'll be seeing some of you twice a week ;)
