HULA is moving into the future with GOOGLE. With our old yahoo accounts failing to serve our purposes any longer, we're retiring the yahoo accounts AT THE END OF AUGUST. So to get the most up-to-date info, please sign up for the googlegroups below.

  • hawaii-ultimate-league-association - (a.k.a. Hulamail) MOST IMPORTANT LIST, official disc-related announcements, mainly for O`ahu (googlegroup)
  • Generalmail - community announcements, non-ultimate such as parties, for sale, etc. (googlegroup)
  • Wahinemail - women's events/discussion (googlegroup)
  • Kanemail - men's events/discussion (googlegroup)
  • Hawaii Ultimate Facebook Group
