Thursday Pre-Kaimana 2000 Party and Carbo Load

Come one come all to the Kaimana 2000 pre-tourney party and carbo load.
Conveniently located for all those playing pickup ultimate at Ala Moana
Beach Par on Thursday night.
 
Where: Snapper's Sports Pub, 1778 Ala Moana Blvd. (across from the Ilikai Hotel)
When: Thursday, Feb. 17, 2000 6:30 p.m. (or shortly thereafter)
Parking: Available at Discovery Bay, just off Hobron (head mauka -
towards the mountains - off of Ala Moana). Or, just leave your car at
Ala Moana and walk. Note: Don't leave valuables in your car and don't
park in the locking parking lot. Snapper's is within easy walking distance from the field.

Why?: Many, many reasons:
    1. Hang out with your favorite ultimate players.
    2. Meet new ultimate players from around the world.
    3. Specially priced spaghetti dinner (meatballs optional)*.
    4. If you're not into spaghetti, choose from the dinner menu.
    5. Happy hour prices to ultimate players all night long* ($1 domestic
      mugs, discounts on well drinks and special prices on over 40 draft
      beers (including many imports and micros) and bottled beers too-
    6. Pool tables, darts, foosball.
    7. Ultimate videos and slide show**
    8. Live band (Red Hot Mamma and the Cow Paddy Daddy's). Band starts early, "just for us". (Band goes on at 8:00)
    9. Contests and prizes.
    10. Outdoor seating is available adjacent to the scenic Ala Wai Boulevard.
* Requires a specially provided "Kaimana 2000" I.D. card. These will be
distributed at pickup (Wed.and Thurs.) and at Snapper's (see KB. Code word - Imua).
** Slide show pending
 
Don't miss this event. It will be a blast!
