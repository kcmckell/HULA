Fees & Bids

  • Player fee is $120 per player*. Fee includes all breakfasts and dinners, entertainment, camping, players pack (cup, disc, and shirt), plus all field supplies (water, fruit, juice, EMT).
  • After 15 January 2004, late registration fee is $150. (Aloha Spirit players are not subject to the late registration fee)
  • Guest fee is $65 (all guests must be registered!)
  • Please make all checks out to 'Kaimana Klassik'.
  • Important Dates -
        • 31 October 2003 Postmark date for all team bids
        15 November 2003 Accepted, wait-listed, and declined teams will be notified
        25 November 2003 Teams must accept/decline bids
        15 December 2003 Half of player fees due (based on up-to-date roster)

        15 January 2004 Deadline for regular registration fee.

  • How to submit an individual bid -
    No team? No problem! We've got Aloha Spirit teams in both the open and womens divisions. Fun and spirited pick-up teams where you'll meet cool people from all over. Nobody gets turned down! To register, send your name, email address, t-shirt size, gender, meal preference, and player fee to:
    Jena Kline/Alex Globerson
    1331 9th Avenue
    Honolulu, HI 96816
  • How to submit a team bid -
    • All bids must include:
      • Contact information for captain or organizer, including name, email address, and phone number(s)
      • The number of players you actually anticipate having on your roster come February, as well as the number you have that are currently committed.
    • Team bids must be postmarked no later than 31 October 2003
    • $300 deposit; U.S. dollars, payable to Kaimana Klassik.
    • We hope your bid will also be fun, original, and convincing! It can be in any format you wish. Let us know what your team will contribute to Kaimana XVII. Send all bids to:
    Buffy Cushman
    2674 Hillside Ave.
    Honolulu, HI 96822
  • Caveats of the bidding process:
      • The $300 initial deposit can later be applied to individual players fees. For those teams that do not make the final cut, we will refund your full deposit no later than 10 December 2003.
      • Bid-winning teams will be notified via email (as will wait-listed teams) by 15 November 2003. Bid-winning teams must respond via e-mail or letter within 15 days of notification, whether or not they wish to accept the bid. If no response is received, that team will forfeit its bid, and the next wait-listed team will be contacted. At this point, the declining bid-winning team will receive a 100% refund on its deposit.
      • Bid-winning teams are required to secure their bid by submitting entrance fees for at least half of their roster (at $120 per player) by 15 December 2004 (postmark date).
      • If a bid-winning and bid-accepting team fails to submit the above mentioned players' fees, its bid will be lost, and the next wait-listed team will be given priority. If we are able to fill this new vacancy, we will issue only a partial deposit refund of $150. If we are unable to fill the vacancy, no refund of deposit will be issued.
      • The remainders of each team's entrance fees are due by 15 January 2004. Any entrance fee postmarked after this date will be assessed the late registration fee of $150.
      • If any team forfeits its position after 1 January 2004, the deposit will be forfeited, regardless of our success in filling the vacancy.


    *Kaimana fees are based on an ideal budget divided by a realistic average of the number of players who have attended the tournament in recent years. Any excess monies earned by the tournament go towards supporting the events, activities, and outreach of the Hawaii Ultimate League Association (HULA).
