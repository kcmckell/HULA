14th Annual Kaimana Klassik

The celebration has ended for another year
(unless your going to Maui this weekend).


Nada Mooger - Open Teams Champions
Nada Mooger beat Grey Tide in a great finals.
The final score was 19-16.
Crisp plays, great spirit by both teams and lots of hucks made for a perfect finale to the tournament.

Shambala - Women's Team Champions
Shambala played the finals against Awiki Na Wahine.
The game was very close and the final score was 17-15.
One of Shambala's players, Amy Dobras, was the big crowd pleaser in the game getting a Hat Trick with three massive gnarly & hairy baps when the score was 15-14. This was truly a great game to watch and I for one was glad that the women's and open finals were staggered .
