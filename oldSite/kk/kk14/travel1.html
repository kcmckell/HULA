Kaimana Travel Tips

Want to book a complete trip? Contact Oaks Travel. They understand the needs of Ultimate players, know Oahu and can take care of all your tournament travel needs. They can also set up your travel arrangements for the Maui Hat Draw on the following weekend.

Of course, you could try booking everything yourself. If you use the Internet then try the search engines. Lycos.com works pretty good. Search for Oahu plus your needs (i.e. Car Rentals, Hostels, Hotels, Bed and Breakfast or Vacation Rentals)
