Aloha Akakiaka,

Kaimana Kaimana IX and the Maui Hat Tournament
have come and gone.and all your old and new family members
in Hawaii want to thank all of you who attended.

Once again the Tiki blessed the Oahu tournament with beautiful
weather, and while sometimes on the brisk side at night, the
game days were always a wonderful playing temperature.

We want to thank all the participants who were wonderful,
gracious, and as always the most loving people we have ever met.
It was truely a blessing to be given the opportunity to share our aloha with
all of you.

Me Ke Aloha Pumehana,
