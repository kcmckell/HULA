Fees

If your check is postmarked via the mail prior to the early registration cutoff date (January 15, 2002), you will qualify for the early registration fee of $120 per person. After the cutoff, which includes registering at the fields, you will owe the regular fee of $150.

So what does $120 get you?


  • A t-shirt, tournament disc, tournament cup, and sticker.
  • 3 Dinner meals - either regular or vegetarian.
  • 3 Breakfasts - including yoghurt, fresh fruits, bagles, cereals, juice, english muffins, and assorted muffins.
  • Entertainment: DJ first night, Hula Dancers and DJ second night, live band third night.
  • Continuous music throughout the tourney.
  • Water, juice, and fruit throughout the day.
  • Camping (OK, camping is normally free, but you wouldn't believe how difficult it is for us to secure 100 campsites at once!)
  • Hmmm, Ultimate?! - At least 8 games of spirited ultimate play per team.
  • An incredibly festive, lively, not-to-be-forgotten experience.

    • And although not included as part of your fee, free-flowing beer for participants over 21 years of age.
    ***NOTE, we will be stricter this year with IDs.
    ***No ID at registration=No beer.

Guests: Guest fees include all of the above, with the exception of ultimate frisbee games and t-shirt. Guest fee:$60.
