Location

The Kaimana Klassik is held on the island of Oahu at the Waimanalo Polo Fields, 41-1200 Kalanianaole Hwy.


There are two ways to get to the Waimanalo Polo Fields:

NO-BRAINER (and scenic) ROUTE:

From the airport (or anywhere in town), get on to H-1 heading East. Do not get off! The H1 Freeway ends and becomes the Kalanianaole Highway. Continue on the road through Hawaii Kai, up past Hanauma Bay, past Sandy Beach, past Sea Life Park, through Waimanalo. The fields will be on your left (right after you pass the McDonald's on the right, keep an eye out for the fields on the left). Campsites at Waimanalo Bay Beach Park are on the right, directly across from the fields.

STILL QUITE EASY, BUT (slightly) FASTER:  

Again, assuming you are coming from the airport, get onto H-1 East. After approximately 2 miles you should see the offramp for the Pali Highway. Get onto the Pali Highway heading North (left at the stoplight). Take the Pali Highway up over the mountains, through the tunnels, and out the other side. Start counting stoplights once you come out of the tunnel. After the third traffic light, merge over to the right lane so that you can make the right at the fourth traffic light. This road is Kalanianaole Highway. Continue along this road for approximately 6 miles. The fields will be on your right; campsites will be on your left.
