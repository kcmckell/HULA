Kaimana Klassik Team Registration Form

The 1998 Kaimana Committee has chose Monday September 29, 1997 (No sooner)as the first day to receive Pre-Bid registrations to be submitted on this page. Only teams who have participated in a past Kaimana will be allowed to bid during this period.

The first 14 Mens and 8 Womens teams to register will be contacted via e-mail and will then have 10 days to send their team deposit of $250. These funds will be used to secure the field and camping facilites. In the event a team fails to send their deposit in a timely manner, their spot will immediately be awarded to the next team on the waiting list, SORRY NO EXCEPTIONS.

Any remining spots will be available for Bid AFTER Monday October 6th, 1997.

All bids shall be awarded on a first come first serve basis, NO EXCEPTIONS

We will update this site weekly.
