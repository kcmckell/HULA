Current Info (updated April 30, 2006)

  • Thank you to everyone who helped to make Kaimana 19 a success! Thanks to all who showed up early to line fields, stayed late to clean up, woke early post-flood to save the tent area from devastation, gave up your Friday morning for the campout, passed on playing to take awesome photos, cheered on local teams from the sidelines, sold t-shirts and discs, carted water to the players with a smile, or did any other task to make Kaimana what it was.
  • Congratulations to Nada Mooger and California Pride, the winners of Kaimana 19! California Pride won in a hard-fought finals against Coyote Ugly for their second consecutive Kaimana win! Seven is the lucky number for Nada Mooger, who beat Doughboy in an intense game to take the title for the seventh straight year! Will they be able to hold onto their titles in Kaimana 20?!
  • Congratulations to Lady Marmalade and Queue , the spirit team winners of Kaimana 19! It was a first to Kaimana for the Texas ladies team and we hope they will return! Once again, the Canadians showed off their spirit and more during the tourney! Thanks for all your positive energy!
  • Please send any comments or pictures you have from Kaimana 19 to us! We would love to hear what you have to say about Kaimana, as well as see any team, game, or evening photos you have to share. Please note that any comments or pictures you send may end up on the website, so only send ones you are willing to share with everyone! Please send an email to kaimanatd@hotmail.com MAHALO!
  • This year was the first installment of the Sunday night slide show. If you are curious what is happening with those photos, please be patient. We are working on making them available to all players. Please check back periodically, as we will be updating the site with more information about the slide show photos.
  • Although it seems far off at this point, Kaimana 20 is coming. Twenty years from a small local tourney at Kapiolani park to the hundreds of world-class players that we have coming out now. The community support has made Kaimana what it is!
  • KAIMANA 20 WEBSITE IS IN THE WORKS. STAY TUNED FOR UPDATES!
