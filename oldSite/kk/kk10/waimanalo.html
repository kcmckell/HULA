Directions to the Waimanalo Polo Fields

From the Honolulu Airport get on the freeway called "H1" going East. Follow this road until you are there (Waimanalo). Along the way the road changes name (to Kalanianiaole Hwy) and reduces to a two-lane road shortly before Hanauma Bay. You'll follow the South coast past Hanauma Bay, and then go around the point and up the East shore. Eight miles after Hanauma Bay you'll pass the Waimanalo McDonalds. One-third of a mile past McDonalds, on the left, is the entrance to the Polo Fields where the games will be held. Across the street from the polo fields is the Waimanalo Bay State Recreation area where camping is provided.
